2014 Annual Report

In 2014, we celebrated 9,950 people who gave their life to Jesus and 5,820 people who took their next step in baptism! We celebrate numbers a lot at NewSpring because we really believe every number has a name, every name has a story, and every story matters to God.

These salvation and baptism numbers, along with the numbers following in this year's annual report, are important because they are connected to people and the life change we're seeing happen in our church.

Jesus tells His disciples in Matthew 28:19-20, "Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you. And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age.‚Äù

We want to see the Gospel move in our state, our country and throughout the world! Jesus is using our church to reach people who are far from Him and I really do believe the BEST is yet to come for our church!
